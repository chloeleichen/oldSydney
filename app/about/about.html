What was it like to live in Sydney in 1885?

Explore Sydney's past from the people who lived here by viewing the 286 photographs taken in the 1800s, or take to the streets for a closer look at the city's unique landscape and history using the markers on the Google Map.

This project is built with Angular and the source code can be found here in Github

Data and Copyright

This web app uses part of a photo collection by the State Library of New South Wales called Photographs of Sydney before 1885. The collection contains over one thousand photographs of Sydney streets, public buildings, views of the Harbour, suburbs etc., chiefly pre 1885. This app selects part of the collection that are clearly dated and can be attributed to an author.
