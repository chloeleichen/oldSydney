Ever wondered what Sydney looked like around 1885?

This app uses part of a photo collection by the State Library of New South Wales called Photographs of Sydney before 1885. The collection contains over one thousand photographs of Sydney streets, public buildings, views in the Harbour, suburbs etc., chiefly pre 1885. This app selects part of the collection that are clearly dated and can be attributed to an author.

The photo used in this app are CC 3.0 and can be accessed from here.
